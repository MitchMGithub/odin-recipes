Time for smores

What you'll need

  • marshmallows
  • graham crackers
  • choclate
  • roasting sticks, can be either wood or metal
  • fire

Step 1 Make a fire

Step 2 Cooking

  1. take two graham cracker halves and put some choclate between them
  2. put a marshmallow on you roasting stick
  3. roast the marshmallow until done
