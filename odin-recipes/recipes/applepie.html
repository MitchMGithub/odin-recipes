Apple Pie, the American classic

Ingredients needed

  • 1 storebought piecrust
  • 6 cups of sliced peeled apples
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8th teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Turn your oven to 425
  2. Place the pie crust in a oven safe pie dish
  3. Mix the remaining ingredients the best you can
  4. Place the pie innards into the dish
  5. Put the top part of the pie on, follow instructions on the pie crust box
  6. Bake for 40 minutes, or until apples and crust is finished
  7. Now you have a pie, enjoy as you see fit
