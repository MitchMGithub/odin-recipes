Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 3/8 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups choclate chips

Baking steps

  1. preheat oven to 375Â°F
  2. combine all the ingredients into a bowl, stir until well mixed
  3. make one inch diameter balls, place on cookie sheet
  4. bake for 10 minutes, or until golden brown, enjoy once finished
